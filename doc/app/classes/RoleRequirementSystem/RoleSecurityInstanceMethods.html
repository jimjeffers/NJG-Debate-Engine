url_options_authenticate?(params = {})

receives a :controller, :action, and :params. Finds the given controller and runs user_authorized_for? on it. This can be called in your views, and is for advanced users only. If you are using :if / :unless eval expressions, 

  then this may or may not work (eval strings use the current binding to execute, not the binding of the target
  controller)
